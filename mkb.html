Murtaza Broachwala

Murtaza Broachwala is the CEO of The Technostic Group. He is also 17. He is aware that this combination raises eyebrows — and finds that fairly useful in his line of work.

He finished school this year with 96% in ICSE and 94% in ISC, scoring full marks in Computer Science every single year of his schooling. His interest in technology was never purely academic: he competed in the CISCE national inter-school robotics championship across all of India, reaching the zonal semifinals, and has spent considerably more time thinking about systems, how they fail, and what sits in the gaps than is typical for someone who was recently doing board exams.

Cybersecurity, as Murtaza sees it, is not a product category. It is a discipline of thinking — one that rewards patience, structure, and the ability to anticipate failure before it happens. He is reasonably good at all three.

The Technostic Group is built on that premise. So is he.
